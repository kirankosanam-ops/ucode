Web Development:

DESCRIPTION:

Web development refers to the work of building, creating, and maintenance of websites. It includes web designing, programming, content publishing, and database management. In simple terms, web development refers to all the work that takes place behind the scenes for a website to look good, work fast and seamlessly. It is the creation of an application that works over the internet i.e. websites and ranges from creating a single plain-text webpage to developing a complex social network. It includes client-side scripting, sever-side scripting, and ecommerce development.
